CASE STUDY

CLIENT Lumina Group
YEAR 2026
SERVICE Corporate Communication

Internal Communication Campaign

A strategic communication campaign designed to improve employee engagement, strengthen organizational culture, and create a more connected hybrid workplace.

Overview

Lumina Group recently adopted a hybrid working model across all departments. While the transition increased flexibility, employee engagement gradually declined due to inconsistent communication, reduced collaboration, and limited interaction between teams.

This project proposes a comprehensive internal communication campaign that strengthens organizational culture, improves information flow, and encourages active employee participation through strategic communication initiatives.

Business Challenge

Low Employee Engagement

Employee engagement scores declined after the transition to hybrid work.

Communication Gap

Company information was delivered inconsistently across departments.

Low Participation

Participation in Town Hall meetings, internal events, and surveys continued to decrease.

Objectives

01

Increase employee engagement by 25% within three months.

02

Improve internal newsletter open rates from 45% to at least 70%.

03

Increase participation in monthly Town Hall meetings and employee forums.

04

Create a consistent communication experience across every department.

Target Audience

Primary Audience

All Lumina Group employees working in hybrid arrangements, including headquarters and regional offices.

Secondary Audience

Department Heads, HR Business Partners, Internal Communication Team, and Executive Leadership.

Communication Strategy

Clear Messaging

Develop one communication framework to ensure every department delivers the same key message across all internal channels.

Two-Way Communication

Encourage employee feedback through pulse surveys, discussion forums, and interactive Town Hall sessions.

Multi-Channel Delivery

Utilize email, intranet, Microsoft Teams, newsletters, and digital signage to maximize communication reach.

Continuous Evaluation

Monitor communication performance monthly using engagement metrics and employee feedback to continuously improve the campaign.

Project Deliverables

The campaign includes a complete set of communication materials prepared to support implementation across all business units.

📰 Internal Newsletter (PDF)

🎤 Town Hall Presentation (PDF)

Reflection

This project demonstrates how strategic communication can improve employee engagement beyond simply distributing information. By combining audience research, stakeholder mapping, integrated communication channels, and measurable KPIs, the campaign provides a structured approach to strengthening organizational culture in a hybrid workplace.
